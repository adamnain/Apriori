preferSimpleBody

fun preferSimpleBody(): AssociationRuleTieBreaker

Adds an additional criteria to the current tie-breaking strategy, which prefers simple bodies over complex ones. I.e., if an association rule contains less items than its counterpart in its body, it is sorted before the other one.
