preferSimple

fun preferSimple(): AssociationRuleTieBreaker

Adds an additional criteria to the current tie-breaking strategy, which prefers simple association rules over complex ones. I.e., if an association rule contains less items than its counterpart in its body and head, it is sorted before the other one.
