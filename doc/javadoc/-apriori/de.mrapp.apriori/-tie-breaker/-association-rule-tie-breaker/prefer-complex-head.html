preferComplexHead

fun preferComplexHead(): AssociationRuleTieBreaker

Adds an additional criteria to the current tie-breaking strategy, which prefers complex heads over simple ones. I.e., if an association rule contains more items than its counterpart in its head, it is sorted before the other one.
